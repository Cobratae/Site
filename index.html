What do you want to know





Jhin is a meticulous criminal psychopath who believes murder is art. Once an Ionian
prisoner, but freed by shadowy elements within Ionia's ruling council, the serial killer
now works as their cabal's assassin. Using his gun as his paintbrush, Jhin creates works
of artistic brutality, horrifying victims and onlookers. He gains a cruel pleasure from putting
on his gruesome theater, making him the ideal choice to send the most powerful of messages: terror.

Here you can find what are Jhin's abilities:

Jhin's passive ability "Whisper" makes him have 4 shots and the 4th one always crits making it deal more damage the lower hp enemies

Jhin's Q ability "Dancing Grenade" allows him to shoot a projectile that can hit up to 4 targets and gains damage each time it kills.

Jhin's W ability "Deadly Flourish" makes him shoot at a straight long line and if it hits an enemy recently
damage by him or an ally, it roots them.

Jhin's E ability "Captive Audience" makes him palce a trap on the the floor that is triggered upon enemy
or a jungle camp enters it's range

Jhin's R ability "Curtain Call" makes him channel and shoot 4 long range shots that slow and deal more damage
the lower hp the enemy is.
